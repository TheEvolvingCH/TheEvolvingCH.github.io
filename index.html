WHO I AM

As the Senior Product Manager at a B2B data & analytics company, I partner across the organization to facilitate development and innovation across multiple product lines. With thousands of buyers, across hundreds of brands and agencies, I strive to create products and services that meet their evolving needs.

A graduate of the University at Albany, SUNY School of Business, I now reside in Westchester County, NY. Outside of work you can find me spending time with my wife and daughters, as well as our Australian Shepherd Bruiser (despite his name he is quite a softie).

On the other days you will likely find me out on the golf course, taking some turns on the ski hill, or on a hiking trail somewhere. While a lot of my hobbies focus on the outdoors, I also enjoy photography, trying out new restaurants and reading about a wide variety of topics. During commutes I'm typically listening to podcasts that educate, ignite curiosity, challenge traditional thinking and encourage people to push themselves.

Experiences to Draw Upon

The more product managers can understand and empathize with the various stakeholders they partner with, or build for, the more successful they will be. Throughout my 10 year career I have held roles on both the sales and buy side of the advertising market. Starting out as a buyer allowed me to gain deep understanding of a client's mindset, executing media buys for major national advertisers. Once on the sales side I was able to focus on strategy, packaging and positioning to best address client needs. It was during this time that I discovered a natural tendency to see gaps and partner across teams to develop solutions. It was this realization that ultimately led me to product management.

No matter the role, my goal is to always remain curious, face challenges head on and try to be better each day.
